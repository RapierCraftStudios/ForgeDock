Global Payments
payments · fx · methods

Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), 80+ local payment methods (SEPA, UPI, PIX, GrabPay), automatic currency conversion at mid-market rates with transparent 0.5% FX spread.

SEPA UPI PIX GrabPay BACS +75 more
Subscription & Usage Billing
billing · meters · invoices

Flat-rate, tiered, per-seat, and metered usage billing. Real-time usage meters with sub-second ingestion. Automated invoice generation, dunning, and retry logic with configurable schedules.

tender.meters.ingest({ event: 'api_call', quantity: 1 })
Marketplace Payouts
connect · transfers · splits

Connect accounts to onboard sellers/vendors. Configurable split rules (percentage, fixed, tiered). Same-day payouts in USD/EUR/GBP. Automatic 1099/W-9 collection and tax reporting.
