What it does

One platform.
Four essential
capabilities.

01
Global Payments

Visa, Mastercard, Amex, SEPA, UPI, PIX, GrabPay, iDEAL, and 75 more local methods. Automatic currency conversion at mid-market rates. A single checkout element that renders the right method for each customer's location.

Cards SEPA UPI PIX FX Conversion
02
Subscription & Usage Billing

Flat-rate, tiered, per-seat, and metered billing models. Real-time usage meters, automated dunning, configurable retry schedules, and prorated upgrades. Invoices generated and emailed automatically.

03
Marketplace Payouts

Connect accounts for your sellers, vendors, or contractors. Configurable split rules — percentage, fixed, or tiered. Same-day payouts in USD, EUR, and GBP. Tax form collection handled.

04
Fraud & Compliance

ML fraud scoring on every transaction. Adaptive 3DS2 authentication. KYC/AML for regulated markets. PCI DSS Level 1 — card data never touches your servers. SOC 2 Type II audited.
