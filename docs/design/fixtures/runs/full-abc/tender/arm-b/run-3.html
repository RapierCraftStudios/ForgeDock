import Tender from '@tender/sdk';
const t = new Tender(process.env.TENDER_KEY);
// 2. Create payment intent in your backend

Capabilities

Everything the money stack needs.

One platform, one contract, one invoice. Tender replaces a patchwork of acquirers, fraud tools, billing engines, and payout networks.

Global Acceptance

Accept cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, UnionPay), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and 80+ local methods including SEPA Direct Debit, ACH, PIX, UPI, Boleto, and iDEAL. Intelligent routing across multiple acquirers maximizes authorization rates by market. Dynamic currency conversion lets customers pay in their local currency while you settle in yours.
135 currencies 47 countries smart routing DCC

Subscription Billing

Model any pricing structure: flat-rate, per-seat, usage-based, or tiered. Proration is automatic on upgrades and downgrades. Trial periods, coupons, and discounts work out of the box. Dunning retries follow customizable schedules. PDF invoices are generated, hosted, and emailed on your behalf. Integrates with revenue recognition workflows for ASC 606 compliance.
metered seat-based proration smart retries invoices

Marketplace Payouts

Split payments between your platform and connected accounts at charge time. Hold funds in escrow with configurable release conditions. Onboard sellers and contractors through a KYC API — no manual process required. Pay out in 47 countries in local currency on instant, daily, weekly, or custom schedules. Batch payouts for platforms handling thousands of recipients.
split payments KYC API escrow instant payouts

Fraud & Risk

Every transaction is scored by an ML model trained on billions of data points across the Tender network. Risk signals include device fingerprint, velocity, behavioral patterns, IP geolocation, and historical merchant data. 3DS2 is applied adaptively — triggered only when risk warrants it, so you don't friction away legitimate customers. Custom rules and block lists can be configured via dashboard or API.
ML scoring 3DS2 adaptive sub-100ms custom rules

Finance Dashboard

A dashboard built for finance and operations teams, not just engineers. Track MRR, ARR, churn, outstanding invoices, dispute status, and payout schedules in real time. Segment revenue by plan, cohort, or geography. Automated dispute response workflows and evidence submission. Tax liability breakdowns by jurisdiction. Export to CSV, QuickBooks, NetSuite, or Xero. Role-based access keeps API keys away from non-technical team members.
