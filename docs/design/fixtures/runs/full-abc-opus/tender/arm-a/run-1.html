Everything the money path needs

From checkout to reconciliation, Tender covers the full lifecycle. No stitching together point solutions. No compliance surprises.

Payments

Accept any payment method

Cards, bank debits, wallets, BNPL, and 50+ local methods. One integration, global reach. Automatic routing picks the cheapest rail.

Billing

Subscriptions and usage metering

Flat, tiered, per-seat, or metered. Proration calculated automatically. Invoices generated and delivered. Dunning retries on your schedule.

Payouts

Move money to anyone, anywhere

Send funds to bank accounts in 45+ countries. Split payments for marketplaces. Real-time payout tracking with webhook notifications.

Fraud

Machine-learned risk scoring

Every transaction scored in real-time using network-wide signals. Custom rules engine for your business logic. 3D Secure triggered adaptively.

Compliance

Regulation handled for you

PCI DSS Level 1, SOC 2 Type II, PSD2 SCA, local licensing. We hold the certifications so you don't manage auditors.

Platform

Multi-party money flows

Connected accounts, delayed settlements, platform fees, 1099-K reporting. Build a marketplace without becoming a money transmitter.

Dashboard

Finance team command center

Real-time revenue, disputes, refunds. Export to your ERP. Role-based access. Search any transaction in milliseconds.

Webhooks

Event-driven architecture

200+ event types with at-least-once delivery. Automatic retries with exponential backoff. Event replay for recovery.

SDKs

Libraries for every stack

Official SDKs for Node, Python, Go, Ruby, PHP, Java, .NET. Typed. Tested. Versioned. Generated from our OpenAPI spec.
