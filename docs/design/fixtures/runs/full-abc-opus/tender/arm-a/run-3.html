Capabilities
Payments

Accept cards, wallets, bank debits, BNPL, and 50+ local methods through a single integration. Intelligent routing picks the cheapest acquirer with the highest authorization rate.

Automatic 3D Secure for SCA-regulated markets. Network tokenization for returning customers. Retry logic on soft declines that recovers revenue without customer friction. 50+ payment methods, 1 API

Subscriptions

Flat-rate, per-seat, tiered, and usage-based models. Proration calculated to the second on mid-cycle plan changes. Automated dunning with configurable retry schedules and grace periods.

Generate tax-compliant invoices with line-item detail. Revenue recognition schedules for your finance team. Trial periods, coupons, and add-ons without custom code. Billing that scales with your pricing model

Payouts

Send funds to bank accounts in 45+ countries. Marketplace split payments distribute revenue to sellers automatically. Real-time payout tracking with webhook notifications at every stage.

Automatic currency conversion at competitive FX rates. Batch payouts for high-volume platforms. Configurable payout schedules: daily, weekly, monthly, or manual. 45+ countries, local rails

Fraud

Machine-learned risk scoring on every transaction using network-wide behavioral signals. Custom rules engine for business-specific fraud patterns. Adaptive 3D Secure triggered only when needed.

Chargeback management with automated evidence submission. Dispute analytics to identify fraud vectors. Radar updates continuously with data from the entire Tender network. ML-powered, real-time, adaptive

Built for finance teams too

Real-time visibility into every transaction, payout, and dispute. Role-based access. Export to your ERP.
