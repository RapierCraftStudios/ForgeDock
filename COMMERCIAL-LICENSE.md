## When You Need a Commercial License

You need a commercial license if:

- You integrate ForgeDock into a proprietary product or internal tool and **cannot** open-source your modifications under AGPL-3.0.
- You build a SaaS or hosted service on top of ForgeDock and **cannot** release your derivative source code.
- Your organization's legal policy prohibits use of AGPL-licensed software in certain contexts.
- You want a support agreement, indemnification, or enterprise terms that the AGPL does not provide.

You do **not** need a commercial license if:

- You use ForgeDock to develop your own projects (it is a development tool, not a library that gets embedded in your product).
- You contribute back your changes under AGPL-3.0.
- Your organization is comfortable with AGPL-3.0 terms.
